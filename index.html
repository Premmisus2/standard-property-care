Got Questions?

FAQ's

Everything you need to know before booking — answered honestly.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), ACH bank transfers, and digital payments via Zelle and Venmo. After every service you'll receive a secure electronic invoice — no paper, no hassle.

Do you have WCB and Insurance?

Yes. Standard Property Care is fully licensed, bonded, and insured in British Columbia. We carry general liability coverage and WCB (WorkSafeBC) on every single job — protecting your property and our team at all times. Proof of insurance is available upon request.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 3–5 business days ahead for standard services. During peak seasons — spring and early fall in Victoria — booking 1–2 weeks in advance guarantees your preferred date. Same-week appointments are often available, so don't hesitate to reach out even on short notice.

Can I set up regular cleaning services?

Absolutely. Our Annual Maintenance Plans are built for homeowners who want consistent, year-round exterior care without the hassle of rebooking. Choose from monthly, bi-monthly, or seasonal visits — we manage the schedule entirely. Plan members save up to 20% on every service and receive priority scheduling.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

No — you don't need to be present for most exterior services. As long as we have access to the property's exterior, gates, and water spigots, our team can complete the job while you're at work or out for the day. You'll receive a full before-and-after photo report directly to your email when the job is done.

Do you offer any guarantees?

Yes — every single service is backed by our 100% Satisfaction Guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with the results, contact us within 48 hours and we'll return to re-service the area at no additional charge. No questions asked, no excuses made. Your satisfaction is our exit condition.
